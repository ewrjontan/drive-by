How It Works

Step 1: Enter Your Location

We'll provide a list of local content creators. View their pricing, reviews and previous work.

Step 2: Choose Your Talent

Choose a content creator that fits your needs. Don't know who to choose? No Problem! Choose from a list of services and your session goals and we will set you up with the perfect candidate.

Step 3: Meet Your Creator

We'll provide you the means to discuss the details of your session. This is where you will determine the date, time and location. You can also discuss your session goals (rolling shots, drone work, track day footage, etc.)

Step 4: Discuss Payment

Determine what the total cost of your session will be. This will be based on the estimated length of your session, multiplied by your content creators hourly rate. If your session goes over the estimated time, adjustments will be made at the time of final payment.

Step 5: Finalize and Book

Go over the final plans of your session. This is it; what you decide on now is what you will get. Your content creator will come prepared with everything necessary to complete your agreed upon terms.

Step 6: Setup Payment

Choose your method of payment (Paypal, venmo or credit card). We will hold your payment for the determined amount. Don't worry, your payment will not be finalized until you have received your digital media and are 100% satisfied with your product and services.

Step 7: Time to Shine

This is it. It's go time. Show up on time to your agreed upon location with a clean vehicle (or dirty, if that's what you're into). Your content creator will arrive half an hour prior to your start time, so be ready to go as soon as you arrive.

Step 8: The Waiting Game

Unfortunately, good things take time. Your content needs to be edited and polished. This can take up to two weeks. You will then receive watermarked proofs of your session.

Step 9: All Done

Let your content creator know that you are satisfied with your proofs and your session. Payment will be finalized and you will receive your final product as digital media. Don't forget to leave review your content creator.

That's it!

If you are not 100% satisfied with your product or your service, let your content creator know when you receive your proofs (Step 8). You can decide if you would like a makeup session or if you want to back out completely. Unfortunately, the time of our content creators is valuable and at this time, we can only provide a partial refund or a discount on a new session with another creator of your choosing. If you would like to report any other problems or issues with your service, please leave a detailed message and we will respond in a timely fashion.
